# Governance

## Decision classes
- Editorial: one maintainer review.
- Benchmark content: two maintainer reviews plus pair-control check.
- Scoring or redline change: two maintainer reviews, public diff and release note.
- Major version: maintainer vote plus public review window.

## Independence
A benchmark author should not be the sole reviewer of a submission using that author's preferred method. External replication reports are preserved even when negative.
