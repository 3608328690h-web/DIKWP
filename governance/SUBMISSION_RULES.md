# Challenge Submission Rules

1. Submit machine-readable traces for all 72 scenarios.
2. Disclose model, prompting, tools, human edits and any benchmark exposure.
3. Do not submit private data, credentials or production actions.
4. Primary leaderboard uses frozen v0.1.0 scenarios and scoring.
5. Manual corrections are allowed only in a separately reported assisted track.
6. Organizers may publish failure analyses and cannot guarantee certification or procurement suitability.
