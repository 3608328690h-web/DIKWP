# 90-Day Release Plan

## Days 1-14: Canonicalization
- Create the canonical repository `DIKWP-PACT`.
- Link AgentMesh, BenchmarkLab and IntentAsset as upstream inspirations; do not duplicate their ZIP blobs.
- Publish v0.1.0-rc1 with issues enabled, Discussions enabled and a GitHub Project board.
- Recruit one benchmark statistician and one external replication partner.

## Days 15-30: Freeze and preregister
- Public review of scenario pairs and redlines.
- Freeze benchmark hash and preregistration.
- Tag v0.1.0 and publish formal Release assets, checksums and citation metadata.

## Days 31-60: External challenge
- Open a 30-day reproduction and submission window.
- Hold two office-hour sessions focused on failure cases, not promotion.
- Require all submissions to disclose benchmark exposure and manual edits.

## Days 61-90: Evidence of life
- Publish the first independent reproduction report.
- Merge at least one external scenario contribution.
- Publish a failure-driven v0.1.1 revision if evidence warrants.
- Submit the paper or workshop abstract.
- Decide whether v0.2 targets real open-weight agents, a Hainan synthetic pilot, or standards mapping.
